Camera Jitter Laboratory

==Purpose==
Experimentation with different camera and gameobject movement techniques, with the goal of eliminating unprofessional camera jitter.

==Tutorial==
(1) Open the "scenes/main" scene.
(2) Run the game.
(3) To fix the jitter on the Rigidbody Sphere, click both buttons once.
(4) Examine each object in the inspector to discover what settings are responsible for minimizing the jitter of the sphere.
